Are we still doing CANopen?

Yes, with one documented deviation:

  • Commands master→slave: SDO expedited download into manufacturer entries 0x2000-0x200B (textbook CiA 301)
  • State slave→master: event-driven TPDO1 on 0x180+nodeId carrying x2001 + x2004 (textbook CiA 301)
  • Slight deviation: a 2 s heartbeat bit is toggled inside x2004 status word to force a TPDO retransmit even when position and running state are unchanged. The CiA-clean way is 0x1800 sub 5 event-timer or a separate heartbeat producer object. See syncModulesToTpdo for details.
